What We Will Do

Data visualization. It should go hand in hand with data analysis. Yet, it doesn't seem to get the attention it deserves. We are the visualizers. We intend to develop tools and techniques to visualize data.

Interact With Your Data

You might need to understand thousands or even millions of rows of data, and, you only have a short time to achieve understanding. Data visualizations can help. The best data visualizations are those that expose something new about the underlying patterns and relationships contained within the data.
